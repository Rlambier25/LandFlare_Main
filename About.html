Who am I?

A realestate investor you can trust

A homegrown business you can trust

I believe in transparency so here it goes. I am just a regular working class guy who has a dream of investing in real estate fulltime. I currently work as a software engineer full-time but I've always had a passion to do more. Real estate has been an interest of mine for as far back as I can remember and getting started in the land buying business was a natural path for me.

It is very important to me that you know I am not a salesmen. I will never pressure anyone or try to sell you on the idea to let me buy your land if you aren't ready. I am looking to buy UNWANTED property from motivated sellers that are ready to exchange their land for cash. So let's make a deal that benefits us both!
