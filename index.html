I RECEIVED A LETTER. NOW WHAT?

If you received a letter from us and signed the enclosed Purchase Agreement, congratulations! Here’s what to do next:

1. SUBMIT YOUR SIGNED AGREEMENT

You can scan or take a picture of your agreement and email it to us at info@tituslandoffers.com. Or you can mail the agreement to 1940 Fountain View Dr. #3063, Houston, TX 77057. If you would like to fax it to us, please email to make arrangements.

2. CONFIRM THE DEAL WITH US

Once we receive your signed Purchase Agreement, we will confirm with you whether we can move forward to close on the property.

3. CLOSE WITH A MOBILE NOTARY

We will draft the necessary paperwork to complete the purchase of your land. We will also hire a notary to meet you at your residence or local meeting spot (like a coffee shop). The notary will present the deed for you to sign and will witness the transaction.

4. GET YOUR MONEY!

Once you sign the deed, the notary will hand you a cashier's check on the spot, for our agreed price. No waiting around for the mail!
